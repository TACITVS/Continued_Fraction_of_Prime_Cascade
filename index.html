Continued Fraction of Prime Cascade

This page displays the constant obtained by the continued fraction whose partial denominators are the prime numbers:

$$ \alpha = [0; p_1, p_2, p_3, \ldots] = \cfrac{1}{p_1 + \cfrac{1}{p_2 + \cfrac{1}{p_3 + \ddots}}} $$

The accompanying LaTeX article outlines a proof that this constant is transcendental.
